Our Travel Company was founded 60 million years ago. Our first customers were cute animals. They were cheerful and funny (sometimes they ate each other for fun). In those distant years, our company was just gaining momentum and we rolled them around all the Galaxies for free as advertising. But one day, one of the passengers chewed through the cables leading to the fusion engine of one of our spaceships and a small explosion occurred. In general, everyone on Earth died out.

**********************************************************************************************

Then, about 2.5 million years ago, our new customers appeared. We called them Almost Homo Sapiens. They could already pay for our tours with crypto stones, which played a big role in the development of our company and allowed us to expand our tours to the limits of other Universes.

**********************************************************************************************

Nowadays we offer you the widest selection of tours to all the Galaxies of our Universe and partially to the nearest Galaxies of neighbouring Universes. Payment is accepted in all currencies of our Universe.
We are sure that You will love Us as much as We love You!
